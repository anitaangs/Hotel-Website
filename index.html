services

All-inclusive

All-inclusive offers a variety of traditional Bulgarian and European dishes which are served in our elegant restaurant. The restaurant of hotel Sunset is with a capacity of 140 people and is divided into a beautiful summer garden and two indoor halls . At the outdoor garden of the restaurant there is a barbecue area where guests can enjoy a show cooking from our Chef.

Pool area

The pool area consists of a big swimming pool, which is divided into a children's part and an adult part. The depth in the children's part is 80 cm, and in the large part it varies from 130 cm to 160 cm at the end of the pool. In the pool area, guests can take advantage of both the poolbar which is meters from the pool and our VIP tents, which are located in our flower garden around the pool. This area is isolated and guests can feel completely at ease and in privacy. The area is large enough to provide the maximum number of our guests with sunbeds and umbrellas to enjoy their holiday around the pool

Housekeeping

Our team takes care of the daily housekeeping and cleaning of the rooms, corridors and common areas of the hotel. By standard, the linen is changed every second day, and the cleaning is performed using the most modern and professional technologies with high-quality detergents.

Bars

All-inclusive bars start at 8:00 and run until 22:30. It includes soda drinks, juice, coffee, tea, hot chocolate. Low alcohol drinks are served until 18:30 - beer, wine and cocktails at the pool bar. After 18:30 hard alcohol is offered - whiskey, vodka, gin, brandy, anise, mint.
