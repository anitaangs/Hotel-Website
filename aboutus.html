For all these years we have welcomed thousands of guests and for the enjoyment of each of them we have invested maximum effort, work and professionalism.

Hotel Sunset is located in the quiet area of Sunny beach and it is just 150m from the beach.It has 73 rooms and 4 apartments. The hotel offers not only the standard breakfast package but also all Inclusive ! It features breakfast, lunch and dinner. The beverage is included in the package! In Hotel Sunset you can enjoy outdoor pool with a beautiful garden, poolside bar with different cocktails and drinks, lobby bar and a garden with children's playground on site. At night you can enjoy our night club which is made in original Irish pub style.

All-inclusive offer a variety of traditional Bulgarian and European dishes which are served in our elegant restaurant. The restaurant of hotel Sunset is with a capacity of 140 places and is divided into a beautiful summer garden and two indoor halls . At the outdoor garden of the restaurant there is a barbecue area where guests can enjoy a show cooking from our Chef.

The hotel is located in a very communicative area. Shops, pharmacies and bus stops are located meters from the hotel.
